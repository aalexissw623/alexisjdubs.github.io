Exercises: Quotations

Quotations include < q > for short quotations, < blockquote > for block quotes, and < abbr > for abbreviatinos.

Here are some examples:

This is a quote. I destroy my enemy when I make him my friend. Abraham Lincoln

This is another quote. With great power comes great responsibility. Uncle Ben from Spiderman

This is a block quote:

This website is made using HTML. I am currently taking an Exploring Computer Science class. The red fox jumped over the lazy brown dog.

This is an abbreviation. The WHO was founded in 1948. Hover over 'WHO' to see the expanded acronym.
