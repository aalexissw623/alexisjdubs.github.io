Introduction

Ever wondering what to eat for your next meal? Your next late-night binge? Need a little spin and a party for your tastebuds? If you want something to chow down on, Zesty 'n' Tasty has got it all.

Zesty 'n' Tasty is mostly a food and restaurant recommendation site, but we do have a recipes page in case you're in to let out your inner chef and get something cookin'. With restaurants in locations all around the United States and a few in Europe and Asia, Zesty 'n' Tasty is a site where you can find food from all over the world.

Here at Zesty 'n' Tasty, we know food isn't all about taste. For a restaurant to be great, service, atmosphere, price, and much more all come into play. From each restaurant, we display a few of our favorite dishes. Prices are labelled with $ being the least expensive and $$$$ as the most. Restaurants are also conveniently labelled by location and country. Please note: Zesty 'n' Tasty does not host any alcoholic foods or beverages.

About The Author

Zesty 'n' Tasty is founded by Alexis Wu, a resident of San Diego and a high school student. Alexis has been a tourist and travelled the world in search for a new taste. In doing so, she has put together a list of her favorite restaurants of all time tailored to your taste.

In her free time, Alexis likes to hike around San Diego and, of course, have a snack or take a break in a local restaurant or shop.
